1. Company Overview & Brand Promise
ShopNest Retail Pvt. Ltd. is a leading multi-category e-commerce platform founded in 2018, headquartered in
Bengaluru, India, with operational hubs in Mumbai, Delhi, and Hyderabad. ShopNest serves over 8 million
registered customers across India, offering more than 2.5 million products spanning Electronics, Fashion,
Home & Living, Beauty, Sports, Books, and Groceries.
Our Mission
To make quality products accessible to every Indian household with a seamless, trustworthy, and delightful
shopping experience — delivered right to the doorstep.
Core Values
 Customer First: Every decision starts with how it benefits the customer.
 Transparency: Honest pricing, clear policies, no hidden charges.
 Speed & Reliability: Fast delivery and dependable service, every time.
 Inclusivity: Products and services accessible to all income groups.
 Sustainability: Eco-friendly packaging and carbon-neutral delivery goals by 2027.

3. Ordering & Checkout Process
How to Place an Order
1. Browse or search for a product using the search bar or category navigation.
2. Select your preferred variant (size, color, storage, etc.) and click 'Add to Cart' or 'Buy Now'.
3. Review your cart — apply coupon codes or use ShopNest Coins if available.
4. Enter or select your delivery address. You can save up to 5 addresses in your account.
5. Choose your preferred payment method and complete the transaction.
6. You will receive an Order Confirmation email and SMS within 2 minutes of successful payment.
Order Modification & Cancellation
Orders can be modified or cancelled within 30 minutes of placement, provided the order has not been picked up
by the logistics partner. To cancel, go to My Orders > Select Order > Cancel Order. For modifications (address
change, quantity), contact support immediately via Live Chat.
Note: Once an order status shows 'Shipped', cancellation is not possible. Customers must initiate a return after
delivery instead.
Bulk & Business Orders
Businesses and institutions can place bulk orders (minimum ■25,000 value) through ShopNest Business.
Benefits include GST invoicing, dedicated account managers, special pricing, and net-30 payment terms for
pre-approved business accounts. Contact business@shopnest.in for onboarding.
Gift Orders
ShopNest offers gift wrapping (■49 per item) and allows customers to include a personalized message card
(free). Gift orders do not include the price invoice inside the package — it is emailed to the buyer's registered
email only.

Tracking Your Order
Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking link via SMS and email. You can also track your order
from My Orders > Track Order. ShopNest partners with Delhivery, BlueDart, Ekart, and India Post for logistics.
Live GPS tracking is available for Same-Day Delivery orders.
Delivery Attempts & Failed Delivery
Our delivery partners make up to 3 delivery attempts. If all attempts fail, the order is returned to the warehouse
and a full refund is issued within 7 business days. Customers may reschedule delivery by contacting support or
updating delivery preferences in the tracking link.
Undeliverable Areas
ShopNest currently cannot deliver to certain remote pin codes (primarily in Andaman & Nicobar, Lakshadweep,
and select northeastern areas). The system will automatically notify customers if their pin code is unserviceable
at checkout.

How to Initiate a Return
1. Go to My Orders and select the item you want to return.
2. Click 'Return/Exchange' and select the reason from the dropdown.
3. Upload photos of the product if it is damaged or incorrect (required for damage claims).
4. Choose your refund method: Original payment source or ShopNest Wallet.
5. Schedule a pickup — a logistics agent will collect the item within 2–4 business days.
6. Once the item passes quality inspection at the warehouse, your refund is processed.

6. Payment Methods & Security
Accepted Payment Methods
 UPI: Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM, Amazon Pay, and all UPI apps via QR or UPI ID.
 Credit & Debit Cards: Visa, Mastercard, RuPay, American Express. No-cost EMI available on select
cards.
 Net Banking: 50+ Indian banks supported including SBI, HDFC, ICICI, Axis, Kotak.
 Cash on Delivery (COD): Available for orders up to ■20,000. COD fee of ■29 applies.
 ShopNest Wallet: Load up to ■10,000. Earn 1% cashback on every wallet payment.
 ShopNest Pay Later: Buy now, pay within 30 days. Available for eligible customers (KYC required).
 EMI Options: No-cost EMI for 3, 6, 9, or 12 months on orders above ■3,000 (bank-dependent).
 Gift Cards: ShopNest gift cards (■100–■10,000) can be used partially or fully at checkout.
 Cryptocurrency: Not currently supported.
Payment Security
ShopNest uses 256-bit SSL encryption for all transactions. Card details are never stored on ShopNest servers
— all card payments are processed through PCI-DSS Level 1 compliant payment gateways (Razorpay and
PayU). Two-factor authentication (OTP) is required for all transactions above ■2,000.
Warning: ShopNest will NEVER call or message asking for your OTP, CVV, or full card number. If you receive
such a call, report it immediately at security@shopnest.in or call 1800-XXX-XXXX.
Failed Payment & Pending Refunds
If a payment fails after deduction, the amount is automatically refunded within 5–7 business days. For UPI, it
typically reverses within 2 hours. If you do not receive the refund after 7 days, contact support with your
transaction reference number.
7. Account Management
Creating an Account
Customers can sign up on ShopNest using a mobile number or email address. OTP verification is mandatory.
Social login (Google and Facebook) is also supported. A single phone number can be linked to only one
ShopNest account.
Forgot Password / Login Issues
To reset your password, click 'Forgot Password' on the login page and enter your registered mobile or email. An
OTP or reset link will be sent. If your account is locked due to multiple failed attempts, it will automatically
unlock after 30 minutes, or you can contact support.

Account Deletion
Customers can request account deletion from Settings > Privacy > Delete Account. Note that pending orders
must be completed or cancelled before deletion. Wallet balance will be refunded to the linked bank account
within 7 business days. Deleted accounts cannot be recovered.

10. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
Orders & Delivery
Q: Can I change my delivery address after placing an order?
A: Yes, but only within 30 minutes of placing the order and before it is shipped. Go to My Orders > Order Details
> Edit Address, or contact Live Chat immediately.
Q: What if I am not available to receive my order?
A: Our delivery partner will make 3 attempts. You can also reschedule via the tracking link sent to your phone.
For high-value orders, an OTP is required at delivery.
Q: Why is my order delayed?
A: Delays may occur due to weather, public holidays, logistics issues, or high demand during sale events. You
can check the latest status on the tracking page. If the delay exceeds the promised date by more than 3 days,
contact support for compensation.
Q: Can I order from outside India?
A: Currently, ShopNest only delivers within India. International shipping is planned for 2026.
Returns & Refunds
Q: My product arrived damaged. What do I do?
A: Do not discard the packaging. Take photos of the damaged item and packaging. Initiate a return within 48
hours of delivery via My Orders and upload the photos. A replacement or full refund will be processed within 5
business days after pickup.
Q: I received a wrong product. How do I report it?
A: Go to My Orders > Report Wrong Item. Provide a photo of what you received. ShopNest will arrange an
immediate pickup and deliver the correct item, or issue a full refund.
Q: Can I return a product without the original box?
A: For Electronics, the original box and all accessories are mandatory for returns. For Fashion, original tags
must be intact. Missing accessories may result in partial refunds.
Q: The seller says the return window has closed, but I received a defective item. What can I do?
A: For manufacturing defects, ShopNest offers an extended 6-month warranty claim process through our
Warranty Support team, regardless of the standard return window.
Payments & Offers

Q: Is it safe to save my card on ShopNest?
A: Yes. ShopNest uses RBI-compliant card tokenization. Your actual card number is never stored — only a
secure token is saved, which cannot be used outside of ShopNest.
Q: Why was my coupon code rejected?
A: Common reasons: the coupon has expired, the minimum order value was not met, the product category is
excluded, or the coupon is for new users only. Check the coupon's terms in My Coupons section of the app.
Q: Can I get a GST invoice for my purchase?
A: Yes. If you add your GSTIN during checkout, a GST invoice will be generated. For past orders, you can
download invoices from My Orders > Download Invoice.
Q: What happens to my ShopNest Coins if I return a product?
A: Coins earned from a returned order are reversed from your Coins balance. Coins used as discount at
purchase are refunded back to your wallet upon successful return.
Account & Privacy
Q: How do I update my phone number?
A: Go to My Account > Profile > Edit. Enter your new number and verify via OTP. Note: each number can only
be linked to one account. If the number is already used, contact support.
Q: How does ShopNest use my personal data?
A: ShopNest collects data to personalize recommendations, process orders, and improve services. Data is
never sold to third parties. You can review ShopNest's full Privacy Policy at shopnest.in/privacy. You can
download your data or request deletion at any time.
Q: Is my ShopNest account activity monitored?
A: For fraud prevention, unusual account activities (multiple failed logins, unusual purchase patterns) may
trigger automatic security reviews. This protects your account from unauthorized access.

11. Escalation & Complaint Resolution
ShopNest follows a 3-tier complaint resolution framework to ensure every customer concern is addressed fairly
and promptly.
Tier 1 — First Response (0–24 hours)
All complaints are first handled by frontline support agents via chat, phone, or email. Most issues (order
tracking, refund status, coupon problems) are resolved at this level.
Tier 2 — Specialist Review (24–72 hours)
Complex cases (disputed refunds, seller misconduct, repeated delivery failures) are escalated to specialist
teams. Customers receive a case reference number and dedicated agent.
Tier 3 — Senior Management (3–7 business days)
Unresolved issues are escalated to the Customer Experience Manager. A written resolution is provided.
Compensation (ShopNest Coins, vouchers, or refunds) may be offered.
External Escalation
If still unresolved, customers can approach the National Consumer Helpline (1800-11-4000) or file a complaint
on the Consumer Disputes Redressal platform at consumerhelpline.gov.in.
