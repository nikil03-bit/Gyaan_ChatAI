# Getting Started with GyaanChat

Welcome to **GyaanChat** — the easiest way to build a custom AI assistant that answers questions from your own documents and knowledge base.

This guide will walk you through everything you need to know to go from a new account to a fully working AI chatbot, step by step.

![GyaanChat Dashboard Overview showcasing the main navigation and stats](/docs-images/dashboard.png)

## What is GyaanChat?

GyaanChat lets you create an AI-powered assistant that is trained on *your* content — not generic information from the internet. You upload your files, personalize the bot, and then embed it on your website. Visitors can then ask natural questions and get accurate, relevant answers — all sourced from your documents.

**No coding knowledge is required** to get started.

## What You Can Do

*   **Upload documents** — PDFs, Word files, text files, and more
*   **Customize your bot** — Set a name, personality, colors, and logo
*   **Test the bot** — Preview exactly what your visitors will see
*   **Embed anywhere** — Add the chat widget to your website with a single line of code
*   **Track conversations** — See what your users are asking and how the bot responds

After logging in, you'll land on your **Dashboard**. Here's a quick tour of the sidebar:

> [!TIP]
> Everything in GyaanChat is isolated to your account. Your documents, conversations, and settings are completely private.

## Your First Steps

Here is the recommended order to set up your bot:

1.  **Upload your documents** — This is your bot's brain. Without documents, the bot has nothing to answer from.
2.  **Customize your bot** — Give it a name and look that matches your brand.
3.  **Test it** — Ask the bot some questions and make sure it's working correctly.
4.  **Embed it on your website** — Copy and paste one line of code.
